Two reasons why linear model is more appropriate for log base 10 mean salaries than for raw mean salaries:

  • Reduced skewness: taking logs compresses the largest values and makes the data more symmetric.

  • Homoscedasticity: the raw salary plot shows increasing spread in later years. Log10 stabilises this variance, making it roughly constant across all years, satisfying the constant variance assumption.
